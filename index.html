📖 How to Play
  • Pick a starting country, then conquer the world.
  • Right-click any foreign country for diplomacy & war actions.
  • Build buildings on your territories by clicking the hotbar (1–7) then clicking your country.
  • 🏙 Cities = +gold/s  | 🏭 Factories = +troops/s  | ✈ Air Bases = planes
  • Justify a war goal → Declare War → Attack to conquer countries.
  • Improve relations (70+) to form alliances. Allies fight alongside you.
  • Research techs to boost attack, defense, gold, or troops by up to +50%.
  • Space = pause  |  WASD / arrows = pan  |  scroll = zoom
About FrontWars

Territorial Wars is a browser-based grand strategy game. Pick a nation and compete to dominate the world map. Build factories, research technologies, forge alliances, and lead your armies to total victory.
